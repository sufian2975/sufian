About Wanderlust

Wanderlust is your ultimate travel planning companion, designed to make trip organization effortless and enjoyable. Our mission is to simplify travel planning so you can focus on the adventure ahead.

With Wanderlust, you can:

  • Plan and organize your trips with our intuitive overview
  • Track expenses to stay within your budget
  • Manage tasks with our to-do list
  • Explore destinations with our interactive map

Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or a month-long adventure, Wanderlust helps you stay organized every step of the way.
